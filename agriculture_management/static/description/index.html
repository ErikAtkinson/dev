Agriculture Management Features

Advanced Dashboard

Dashboard displays fixed data: Lands/Farms, Farmers, Farming Seasons, Crops, Stocks, Incidents; with user-friendly graphs.

Farm Types

Choose farm types tailored to your preferences and requirements from a diverse selection.

Farming Seasons

Improve year-round planning and execution through effective agricultural management strategies.

Season Crops

Feature aids in managing and tracking crop cycles, optimizing yield and resource allocation throughout the seasons.

Crop Healthy Manures

Maintain crop health with appropriate manures throughout the agriculture season, fostering robust growth and yield optimization.

Crop Fertilisers

Effectively oversee crop-specific fertilizer needs, including costing, vendor relations, charge tracking, and specialized billing for efficiency.

Crop Seeds

Manage seeds by tracking costs, vendor relations, charge monitoring, and generating vendor-specific bills for agricultural season.

Resources

Effectively supervising agronomists, field managers, agriculture fleets, equipment, employees, farm animals, and workers.

Season Budgets

Manage budgets seasonally to plan and allocate resources effectively for various agricultural seasons, enhancing productivity and efficiency.

Profit & Loss

Track income and expenses per season for effective financial analysis and decision-making in agricultural operations.

Reports

Manage and display seasonal Excel and PDF budget reports, enabling comprehensive data analysis and agricultural reporting.

Lands / Farms

Manage lands, farmers, farm locations, fleets, animals, and farming seasons effortlessly.

Farmers

Pick from diverse farmers to align with your distinct requirements and personal preferences.

Field Visits

Farm inspections and monitoring throughout the agricultural season to boost productivity and informed decision-making.

Crop Diseases

Manage crop diseases effectively throughout the agriculture season, ensuring optimal health and productivity for your crops.

Cropping Types

Utilize diverse cropping methods to optimize land usage and enhance crop diversity for efficient farming practices.

Crop Pesticides/Herbs

Manage pesticides per crop, covering costing, vendor relations, charge tracking, and vendor-specific billing for agricultural efficiency.

Crop Incident Types

Categorize and manage different types of crop incidents to address challenges and ensure agricultural productivity.

Crop Productions

Optimize inventory control and organization by efficiently managing crop production stocks and storage locations.

Season Expenses

Seasonal expenses encompass labor, pesticide, fertilizer, seed, equipment, fleet, electricity charges, miscellaneous costs, and more.

Projects & Tasks Management

Efficiently manage agricultural activities by creating separate projects and tasks for different seasons, enhancing organization.

Analysis Reports

Track budget versus actual expenditures seasonally, alongside soil, weather, and water analysis reports for comprehensive management.
